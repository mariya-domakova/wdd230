Design Principles Document

Hick's Law

W3Schools website

Hick's Law is applied on this platform, which offers a wide range of programming languages and other skills for learning. To prevent overwhelming the user, the top of the page includes only the most popular languages in the navigation menu, along with a search field.

Fitt's Law

IKEA website

Fitt's Law' is demonstrated through the use of a large yellow area that encompasses the 'Go Shopping' button. Users can easily navigate to the shopping section without the need to precisely target the button; simply hovering over this yellow area activates the transition.

Repetition

TripAdvisor website

The repetition principle is evident on the TripAdvisor website, where consistent design elements, such as fonts, colors, and layout, are used across various pages. This creates a cohesive and familiar user experience, making navigation and information retrieval more intuitive.
